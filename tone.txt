Content Tone & Voice System Template
====================================

This file is a template for defining your organization's brand voice and tone guidelines.
Replace the content below with your own brand persona, voice pillars, and communication guidelines.

1. The Brand Persona
Define who you are and your core identity.

2. Core Voice Pillars
Establish the key principles that anchor your content.

3. The Tone of Voice Scale
Define where your brand sits on scales like:
- Confident vs Humble
- Professional vs Casual
- Visionary vs Tactical
- Emotional vs Analytical

4. Communication Guidelines by Audience
Define how to communicate with different audience types.

5. Vocabulary & Syntax Rules
List preferred words, phrases, and sentence structures.

6. Case Study Framework
Define how to present work and projects.

7. Practical Application: "Dos and Don'ts"
Provide examples of correct vs incorrect tone.

8. Visual/Audio Cues
Note any visual or sensory elements that inform your writing style.
